ABOUT

Davao del Norte State College is a public college in New Visayas, Panabo City,
Philippines which provides instruction and progressive leadership in education, engineering, arts,
sciences, fisheries, and other fields.

Established:

February 14, 1995 (as Davao del Norte State College)
April 29, 1969 (as Davao del Norte School of Fisheries)

College Presidents:

Joy M. Sorrosa (2019-Present)
Jonathan A. Bayogan (December 2010–2018)
Edgardo M. Santos (2001–2010)
Vicente Hermoso (1995–2001)

Main Campus:

New Visayas, Panabo City

Extension Campuses:

Datu Jose A. Libayao, DNSC Carmen Annex
